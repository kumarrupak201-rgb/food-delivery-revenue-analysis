# 🍔 Food Delivery Platform — Revenue & Operations Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
End-to-end data analysis of **21,321 real food delivery orders** 
across multiple restaurants and cities (Delhi NCR) on an 
aggregator platform similar to Swiggy/Zomato.

This project simulates the exact work done by a 
**Revenue Associate** at a food-tech company — 
discount analysis, store benchmarking, funnel tracking, 
and campaign performance monitoring.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Questions Answered
- Which restaurants generate the highest revenue?
- Do discounts increase order volume and AOV?
- What is the order fulfillment funnel (Delivered vs Cancelled)?
- Which months/days have peak order volumes?
- Which subzones/cities are top performers?
- How does Kitchen Prep Time affect customer ratings?

## 🔑 Key Findings

- 🏆 **Top Revenue Restaurant:** Aura Pizzas
- 📦 **99.1% Delivery Rate** — extremely high fulfillment
- 🏷️ **Discounted orders** show higher AOV than 
  non-discounted orders
- ⏰ **Peak ordering hours** identified for ad optimization
- 📅 **December** shows highest monthly revenue spike
- ❌ Top cancellation reasons identified and 
  recommendations provided

---

## 💡 Recommendations

```
1. Focus ad spend during peak hours for maximum ROI
2. Optimize discount constructs — 
   Gold & Promo discounts drive highest conversions
3. Reduce cancellation rate by addressing 
   top rejection reasons
4. Improve KPT for low-rated stores
5. Run targeted campaigns on low-traffic days
6. Expand in top-performing subzones
```
